Instructions to Download apps from Android AppStore :-
1) Click on the App Link and select "Save File" to download the APK File of the app of your choice .
2) Once downloaded , browse to the location where you've previously downloaded the APK File .
3) Click on the APK File to install the app on your Android Device .
4) All set ! Now you can use the app !
